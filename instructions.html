4) Invoke the spirit of your loved one by repeating the following: “We are gathered here to contact the spirit of XX. The spirit will communicate by typing his answers on the screen.

5) Make sure that the spirit you are talking to, is the spirit of the person you were looking to reach. Remember, this is an open bridge, and others could try to use it too. Ask personal questions that only that person could answer.

6) Never ask a spirit to manifest itself through other objects. All communication should only be made through this application.

7) If at some point you wish to end the conversation, say goodbye three times and wait until the application closes the communication with the after world. Do not log out until the application indicates that you're allowed to disconnect.
